# Licensing (Public Overview)

MakArio is modular. Licensing is per module.

## AI Judge (first commercial module)
Possible delivery formats:
1) **SDK** — integrates into partner apps
2) **SaaS** — evidence pipeline as a service
3) **Arena Installation** — on-site setup for events

AI Judge sells **evidence infrastructure**:
- clips (~3s)
- metadata
- hashes/identifiers
- durable delivery

## Governance layer
Manager defines policies:
- consent
- export/share boundaries
- device hub governance
- legal/tax templates (jurisdiction-adaptable)
