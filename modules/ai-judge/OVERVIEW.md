# AI Judge — Product Overview (3 minutes)

AI Judge is an evidence-pipeline module for boxing judging:
- proof clips (~3s)
- structured metadata
- hashes/identifiers
- durable event queue & delivery

AI Judge is a source of facts, not decisions.

> Proprietary biomechanical vision system based on non-standard control points

Implementation details are protected and not disclosed publicly.
